Content Writing

Ever since I was very, very young, I've been a writer. From fiction to research to casual blog posts, I've felt driven to document all I've come across. I love telling a compelling story with words, something that draws you in and convinces you of my message. My father was a self-described "SEO Wizard", and I would spend countless hours typing out posts, content and social media pages for him (at pennies an hour, I might add).

My dream with my now complete AA in English was to become a content writer for local businesses, which was swiftly met with the reality that freelance work is a competitve and harsh place. Heartbroken, I reached out to my skillset and tried to find a career filled with more tangible goals. There I met my love of coding, which has propelled me into the path I am currently on and was always meant for. This new path, however, squelched none of my desire to write, communicate, and sway the mind of the reader.

There is no writing job too big, too complicated, too challenging. I want to do your hardest technical writing jobs explaining the most vague and hard-to-convey-concepts. Let me prove to you and your business that I live to excell and whatever job I am given, and the task of thrilling and informing the reader is one I am ready and willing to take.
